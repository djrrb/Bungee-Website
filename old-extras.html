Extras

Since Bungee is only intended to be used for a few words at a time, a single letterform can make a huge difference. Bungee comes with a handful of alternate characters that can help you fine-tune the look and feel of your text. These include a serifless I and L, a smaller, less-obtrusive apostrophe, and a set of rounded, retro caps that are reminiscent of Art Deco signs. More about Bungee’s alternates »
